myMBTA

Inspired by the 60+ overlapping, third-party MBTA mobile/web applications, these wireframes represent myMBTA, a theoretical "do-it-all" mobile app for the MBTA -- a versatile resource for any and all MBTA needs. The design identity was largely inspired by ProximiT©, an existing MBTA app. This was an early opportunity for me to explore mobile, user interface design.

Including advertisements will generate revenue for the MBTA to help maintain its infrastructure.

The middle frame (Subway) was pulled directly from a screenshot of ProximiT© and then adapted.

Map created with mapbox.com and inspired by Google Maps.

Right frame (Safety) inspired by the MBTA See Say mobile app.
