Design presents me an approach to solving problems, development presents me the tools to get the job done.

I'm a second year student at Northeastern University studying computer science and interaction design. I'm passionate about the intersection of art and technology and aim to expand my understanding of effective design.

With a growing background in computer science, my goal is to harness development as a tool for reaching solutions. I stretch across two powerful disciplines, each with the potential to effect change and solve problems.

My interests lie in UI/UX design, human computer interaction, and full-stack development along with typography and data visualization.

While most of my experience thus far has been in design, my focus now is on development. I'm eager to learn new skills and take on new challenges as a designer-developer in the making.
